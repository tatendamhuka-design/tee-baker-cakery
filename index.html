❤️ Baked With Love, Every Single Day

Welcome to Tee Baker Cakery — where every cake is baked with passion, precision, and a whole lot of love. Founded by Thobeka Dlomo, our bakery started as a home-based dream and has grown into a beloved cake destination for families across Amanzimtoti and Durban South.

We specialize in custom-designed celebration cakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, baby showers, and every milestone. Plus we offer delicious scones, snowballs, and cupcakes in bulk sizes (5L, 10L, 20L) — perfect for parties, events, and corporate functions.

Our promise: No mass production, no frozen goods. Each order is freshly baked, hand-decorated, and prepared exclusively for you.

“We believe every celebration deserves a treat as special as the moment itself. That’s why we pour our heart into every cake, cupcake, snowball and scone.” — Thobeka (Owner & Head Baker)
